The Pixel 7 Pro is a high-end Android smartphone developed by Google. It was released in October 2022 and features a 6.7-inch OLED display with a resolution of 1440 x 3200 pixels. The phone is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 895 processor and comes with 12GB of RAM and up to 1TB of internal storage. The Pixel 7 Pro has a triple-camera setup on the back, consisting of a 108-megapixel primary camera, a 12-megapixel ultra-wide camera, and a 5-megapixel telephoto camera. The front-facing camera is 32 megapixels. Other features of the Pixel 7 Pro include 5G connectivity, an in-display fingerprint sensor, and a 5000mAh battery with fast charging capabilities.
The Pixel 7 Pro has dimensions of 163.5 x 75.8 x 8.9 mm (6.44 x 2.98 x 0.35 in) and weighs approximately 209 g (7.37 oz). It features a large 6.7-inch OLED display with a resolution of 1440 x 3200 pixels and a 120Hz refresh rate. The phone has thin bezels and a small punch-hole cutout for the front-facing camera, which allows for a high screen-to-body ratio and a more immersive viewing experience. Despite its large screen size, the Pixel 7 Pro is designed to be comfortable to hold and use with one hand.
